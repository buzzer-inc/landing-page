Strive is Buzzer’s intentional commitment to level the playing field for Black communities and communities of color who are underserved, underrepresented, and underinvested, leveraging the technology of sport and 1% of the company. 1% equity manifests itself in a) access to resources and, b) meaningful contributions to organizations committed to sustainable change, equitable hiring practices, etc.

Additionally, 1% of net profits will be donated to organizations with aligned commitments to reinvest into these communities. Lastly, our community of customers will be empowered to donate 1% of their micropayment to partnering organizations to maximize our impact.

Mentorship Program

Strive Mentorship Program aims to connect Black students enrolled in HBCUs with experienced individuals across a variety of industries. Through six facilitated sessions, Mentees and Mentors will discuss topics ranging from professional/career goals, leadership qualities, to identifying strengths and weaknesses.
